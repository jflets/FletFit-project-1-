About us

FletFit is a high-performance gym located in Haywards Heath, West Sussex, UK. Our state-of-the-art facility and experienced trainers are dedicated to helping you achieve your fitness goals.

We offer personalized training plans and cater to all fitness levels, from strength training to functional fitness. Our gym provides a welcoming and supportive environment, with top-of-the-line equipment and ample space.

We also offer group classes and personal training sessions to keep you motivated and on track. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting your fitness journey, FletFit is the perfect place to improve yourself and reach your goals. Join our community of like-minded individuals committed to living their best lives.
